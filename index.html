About this database

This is a searchable, mobile- and desktop-friendly atlas of human-infective viruses. Each record includes viral taxonomy (ICTV), genome type, envelope status, likely transmission routes, observed host range, and where and when the virus was first described. Where a match exists, we also show a spillover risk score — an estimate of a virus's potential to spread from animals to humans.

Data sources

  • Zhang et al. — Human-infective RNA virus database (taxonomy, transmission, host range, discovery data).
  • SpillOver: Viral Risk Ranking — spillover.global (spillover risk scores).
  • WHO — Pathogens prioritization for research and development.
  • CEPI 3.0 Strategy — pandemic preparedness context.

For research and educational use. Data is provided "as is" from the sources above and may contain errors or omissions; always confirm against primary literature before relying on it. Not medical advice.
